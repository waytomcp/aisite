## Company Profile: MovingHead.net (Guangzhou Xiangming Stage Lighting Equipment Co., Ltd.)

### Industry Overview

MovingHead.net, officially operating as **Guangzhou Xiangming Stage Lighting Equipment Co., Ltd.**, is a professional manufacturer in the **stage and entertainment lighting industry**, specializing in the research, development, manufacturing, and global distribution of intelligent stage lighting equipment. The company operates within the professional lighting segment serving live performances, theaters, television studios, commercial events, weddings, houses of worship, and entertainment venues.

The global moving head and intelligent stage lighting market has experienced steady growth driven by increased demand for live entertainment, immersive visual experiences, and high-efficiency LED technologies. Within this context, MovingHead.net positions itself as a **cost-effective, high-reliability professional lighting solution provider**, bridging the gap between premium international brands and emerging-market demand.

---

### Products and Solutions

MovingHead.net offers a comprehensive portfolio of professional lighting products, including:

* **LED Profile / Framing Moving Head Lights**
* **Beam Moving Head Lights (260W / 400W / 480W / 500W series)**
* **Beam-Spot-Wash Hybrid Moving Heads**
* **LED Wash and Zoom Wash Moving Heads**
* **Follow Spot Lights**
* **PAR Lights and Studio Lighting Fixtures**

All products are designed to support standard DMX512 control protocols and advanced lighting programming requirements. The company places strong emphasis on optical performance, brightness consistency, thermal management, and long operational lifespan.

Several flagship LED profile moving head models deliver **over 50,000 lumens**, representing a **brightness improvement of approximately 40–45% compared to earlier-generation fixtures**, while reducing overall unit weight by **15–18%**, significantly improving installation efficiency and transport costs for rental companies.

---

### Company Location and Facilities

The company is headquartered in **Guangzhou, China**, with its manufacturing and R&D base located in **Huadu District**, close to Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport. The facility covers approximately **8,000 square meters**, integrating R&D laboratories, production lines, aging test zones, and quality inspection departments. This location provides strong logistical advantages for both domestic distribution and international exports.

---

### History and Development

Founded in **2010**, MovingHead.net has more than **a decade of experience** in the professional lighting industry. Since its inception, the company has followed a vertically integrated development strategy, controlling product design, assembly, quality testing, and after-sales service.

Over the years, MovingHead.net has expanded its global footprint, exporting products to **more than 50 countries and regions** across Europe, North America, South America, Southeast Asia, the Middle East, and Africa. Domestically, the company has established **over 30 service and support centers across China**, ensuring fast response and localized technical support.

The company has obtained multiple international certifications, including **ISO 9001 (Quality Management)**, **ISO 14001 (Environmental Management)**, as well as **CE and RoHS compliance**, meeting the regulatory and safety standards of major international markets.

### Core Competitive Advantages

**1. Industry-Leading After-Sales Service Policy**
MovingHead.net distinguishes itself by offering a **three-year warranty and after-sales service policy**, which is significantly longer than the industry norm of 12 months. This extended warranty reduces lifecycle maintenance costs for customers by an estimated **30–40%**, particularly for rental companies operating high-frequency equipment.

**2. Proven Performance Improvements**
Through continuous R&D investment, the company has achieved measurable performance gains:

* **Up to 45% increase in luminous output**
* **15–18% reduction in fixture weight**
* **Improved thermal efficiency extending LED lifespan by approximately 25%**
  These improvements directly translate into higher operational efficiency and lower total cost of ownership.

**3. Strong Manufacturing and Quality Control**
An integrated production system and standardized quality testing processes ensure consistent product reliability. Internal testing indicates a **failure rate reduction of approximately 35%** compared to earlier production cycles, enhancing long-term stability in demanding live-event environments.

**4. Global Export Capability**
With products distributed to over **50 international markets**, MovingHead.net has built extensive experience in meeting diverse regulatory, voltage, and application requirements, positioning itself as a reliable global supplier.

### Core Limitations and Challenges

Despite its strengths, MovingHead.net faces certain challenges:

* **Brand recognition in high-end Western markets** remains lower than that of long-established premium brands such as Martin, Robe, or Clay Paky.
* **Advanced software ecosystems and wireless control integrations** are areas requiring continued investment to compete at the very top tier of the professional lighting market.

These challenges represent opportunities for future growth rather than structural weaknesses.
